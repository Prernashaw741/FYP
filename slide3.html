Current Challenges

Key Pain
Points

The traditional approach to research management creates friction at every level, from individual faculty to institutional leadership.

The Core Issue

Disjointed systems lead to a significant gap between actual research output and reported metrics.

Manual Uploads & Fragmentation

Faculty must manually enter data into multiple portals, leading to fatigue and resistance.

Administrative Burden

Compiling reports for NAAC, NIRF, and NBA takes months of manual reconciliation.

Months Wasted

Valuable research time is lost to data entry and verification instead of innovation.

Duplicate Entries & Errors

Lack of a centralized version control leads to inconsistent and inaccurate records.

Underreported Impact

Without real-time tracking, many achievements go unnoticed in global rankings.
