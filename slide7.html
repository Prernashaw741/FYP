Key Features & Functionalities

A comprehensive suite of tools designed to automate, analyze, and optimize institutional research workflows.

DOI Automation

One-click metadata fetching from Crossref. Automatically links authors and populates publication details.

OCR Processing

Intelligent text extraction from scanned award certificates and PDFs with confidence scoring.

SDG Mapping

Auto-classification of publications into UN Sustainable Development Goals to track social impact.

Role-Based Access

Tailored dashboards for Faculty, HODs, and Administrators ensuring relevant data visibility.

Duplicate Detection

Advanced algorithms to identify and merge duplicate records, maintaining a clean dataset.

Real-Time Reporting

Live visualization of citations, funding, and publication trends for instant strategic insights.

Compliance Exports

Pre-formatted data exports for NAAC, NIRF, and NBA accreditation bodies, saving months of work.
